# Application: Headspace: Mindful Meditation
- Most important difference between pipelines: The manual pipeline is the most detailed, the automated pipeline is the fastest, and the hybrid pipeline sits in the middle because it keeps some manual refinement while still saving time.
- Most useful pipeline: The hybrid pipeline was the most useful overall because it stayed fully traceable and testable while still being more focused than the automated version.
- Most surprising finding: The automated pipeline got perfect traceability and testability scores even though some of its personas and requirements still felt more general than the manual and hybrid ones.
- Observed weakness in the automated pipeline: Some automated personas combine too many different complaints into one user type, so the final requirements are not always tied as tightly to the review evidence.
